Winter Olympic

The Winter Olympic Games are a major international multi-sport event held every four years for sports practiced on snow and ice.

They are a celebration of winter sports excellence, showcasing athletes from around the world.

How to Enjoy to Watch 6 Main Sports in Winter Olympic?

Figure Skating

It will be even more exciting to watch the athletes perform aerial spins while maintaing their facial expressions!

Ice Hockey

Watch the players keep up with the fast pace of the game at speeds of over 160km/h on the ice while controlling the puck!

Nordic Combined

Watch the incredible movements of the athletes who must perform both ski jumping, which requires explosive jumping power, and cross-country skiing, which demands great endurance!

Ski Jumping

It will be really exciting to watch athletes fearlessly jump of the ramp relyng only on thier skis!

Speed Skating

Explosive speed on the straight course is important, but also watch the strategic battle between the athletes!

Cross-Country Skiing

As it is often called the marathon on snow, watch the athletes push their poles on the uphill without giving up, showing incredible endurance and mental strength!

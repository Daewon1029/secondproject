Summer Olympics

The Summer Olympic Games, also known as the Summer Olympics or the Games of the Olympiad, is a major international multi-sport event held once every four years. The inaugural Games took place in 1896 in Athens, Greece, and the most recent was held in 2024 in Paris, France. These Games bring together athletes from around the world to compete in various sports, fostering international unity and sportsmanship.

BaseBall

Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of nine players each, where the objective is to score runs by hitting a ball and running around a series of bases.
